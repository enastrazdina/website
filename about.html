Vika is a fashion and lifestyle photographer, content creator and visual thinker born and currently based in Riga, Latvia. Her photography style is strong, both feminine and masculine; sometimes mysterious and with a cinematic touch. Her clients are Madara Cosmetics, M Couture, Benji Knewman, Zuzeum, L'Officiel Baltics, Dr. Hauschka, Pastaiga, Pranamat, Stila Grāmata, Katya Katya London, Riga Fashion Week, Pixie Won't Play, Room 88.
She appears in/has made photo serie collaborations for COS, Verso, Creme de la Creme, Chimi Eyewear, Capital Clinic Riga, Kiehl's, Superga, Mini Latvia, Amomento, Abavas Vīni, Teva, H&M, Ecco Shoes, Karen Millen, Levis, Lindex, Paulig, Supernormal People, Apart from photography she has a strong desire for interior design, culture and other things.
